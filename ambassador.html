Thank you for registering as the Student Ambassador in ABACUS. ABACUS is now bigger and better! This is your chance at being a pioneer. Stand out and lead the crowd.

Who is a Student Ambassador?

Student Ambassadors are the “official ABACUS representatives" in their respective colleges. The student ambassador spreads the information and updates about “Abacus'16" in their respective colleges.

Responsibilities

  • Bring in a workshop participation of minimum 5 students from your college to become a Student Ambassador.
  • Receive marketing materials (Posters etc.,) and display them at prominent places in their campuses thereby popularizing the events.
  • Co-operate with the Event Organizers of Abacus.
  • Create an awareness regarding the opportunities available at Abacus'16 to all students in their campus.
  • Promote your college student’s participation in Abacus’16 events and workshops.
  • Participate in Abacus’16 onsite events.

Benefits

  • Free Registration for all events of Abacus.
  • Free T-shirts will be provided for onsite participation.
  • Official Certificate from the Computer Society of Anna University (CSEA).
  • If you bring in a workshop participation of at least 10 students from the campus , then you can attend one workshop for free.
  • If you bring in a workshop participation of at least 20 students from the campus, then you can attend two workshops for free where you have an option of referring your friend for one of the workshop.
  • “Best Student Ambassador” Award will be given based on various criteria and Quality maintained during the course of the Abacus'16.
  • Chance to learn marketing and branding by practicing it in their college campuses. Please send your complete postal address for sending the marketing details.
